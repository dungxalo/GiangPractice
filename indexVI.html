Meet Dasion.


At Dasion we are utilizing novel Geometric Unified Learning (GUL) to bring fast, interpretable decision making tools to healthcare practitioners, reinventing the relationship between AI and medicine. We build systems that expedite health data interpretation and diagnosis, allowing healthcare professionals to spend more time doing what they do best, caring for their patients.

Dasion gặp gỡ


At Dasion we are utilizing novel Geometric Unified Learning (GUL) to bring fast, interpretable decision making tools to healthcare practitioners, reinventing the relationship between AI and medicine. We build systems that expedite health data interpretation and diagnosis, allowing healthcare professionals to spend more time doing what they do best, caring for their patients.

Our Technology

Dasion's Geometric Unified Learning (GUL) combines the power of deep learning with ideas from differential geometry, creating a robust, highly-visual machine learning paradigm. GUL is optimally-suited to healthcare applications as it can easily handle healthcare-level data volumes and provides unparalleled transparency in decision making.
